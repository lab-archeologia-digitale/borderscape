# Bibliography

This document contains the extended bibliography which has been used in the database. Entries are listed in alphabetical order.

Periodicals have been abbreviated as follows:
- **Antiquity**: A Review of World Archaeology
- **Archéo-Nil**: Revue de la Société pour l’Étude des Cultures Prépharaoniques de la Vallée du Nil
- **AZANIA**: Archaeological Research in Africa
- **INORA**: International Newsletter on Rock Art
- **JARCE**: Journal of the American Research Center in Egypt
- **JEA**: Journal of Egyptian Archaeology
- **MDAIK**: Mitteilungen des Deutschen Archäologischen Instituts, Abteilung Kairo
- **TdE**: Trabajos de Egyptología
- **ZÄS**: Zeitschrift für Ägyptische Sprache und Altertumskunde
